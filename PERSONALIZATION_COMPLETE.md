## How to Customize Further

### Update Your Story
1. **Hero Section:** Edit the heading and intro in `components/hero.tsx`
2. **About Section:** Modify your background in `components/about.tsx`
3. **Journey Section:** Update milestones in `components/coding-journey.tsx`

### Update Projects
1. Open `components/projects.tsx`
2. Add your real project details:
   - Problem you solved
   - Solution implemented
   - What you learned
   - Tech stack
   - Live link and GitHub repo

### Add More Sections
The modular component structure makes it easy to add:
- Blog section
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Services offered
- Speaking engagements
